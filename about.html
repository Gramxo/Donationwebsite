About Save the Kids

We are dedicated to improving the lives of children around the world through education, healthcare, and community development.

Our Mission & Vision

Learn about what drives us and our commitment to creating a better future for children everywhere.

Our Mission

Save the Kids is committed to ensuring that every child has access to education, healthcare, nutrition, and protection from harm. We work tirelessly to create sustainable solutions that address the root causes of poverty and inequality.

Through partnerships with local communities, governments, and other organizations, we implement programs that create lasting change in the lives of children and their families.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child has the opportunity to thrive, regardless of where they are born or their circumstances. A world where children's rights are respected, their voices are heard, and their potential is realized.

We believe in a future where no child suffers from preventable diseases, goes to bed hungry, or is denied an education. Together, we can make this vision a reality.

Our Story

How Save the Kids began and our journey to help children worldwide

From Small Beginnings to Global Impact

Save the Kids was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate individuals who witnessed firsthand the challenges facing children in developing countries. What began as a small initiative to provide educational supplies to a single school has grown into a global organization that impacts the lives of thousands of children each year.

Our founders believed that every child deserves the chance to reach their full potential, regardless of where they were born or their circumstances. This belief continues to guide our work today as we expand our programs and reach more communities in need.

Over the years, we have evolved our approach based on evidence and experience, focusing on sustainable solutions that empower communities to create lasting change for their children.
