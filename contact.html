Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our organization and how you can help

How is my donation used?

Your donation directly supports our programs that help children in need. We allocate funds to education, healthcare, nutrition, and child protection initiatives. At least 85% of all donations go directly to program services, with the remaining funds used for essential administrative costs and fundraising efforts to expand our reach.

Can I volunteer with Save the Kids?

Yes! We welcome volunteers who want to contribute their time and skills. We have opportunities both locally and internationally, ranging from administrative support to direct program involvement. Visit our Volunteer page to learn more about current opportunities and how to apply.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Save the Kids is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a tax receipt for your donation that you can use for tax purposes.

How can I organize a fundraiser for Save the Kids?

We appreciate your interest in organizing a fundraiser! You can host various types of events, from bake sales to charity runs. Visit our Fundraise page for ideas and resources, or contact our fundraising team at fundraising@savethekids.org for personalized support and guidance.

Can I sponsor a specific child or project?

Yes, we offer child sponsorship programs and project-specific funding opportunities. Through child sponsorship, you can support a specific child's education, healthcare, and overall well-being. For project sponsorship, you can direct your donation to a specific initiative or location. Contact our donations team for more information.
