Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donating to Seelife

Is my donation secure?

Yes, your donation is completely secure. We use industry-standard encryption technology to protect your personal and financial information.

Do I get a receipt?

Yes, we provide receipts for all donations. You will receive an email receipt immediately after your donation, and we can also mail you a printed receipt if requested.

Is this donation tax deductible?

Yes, Seelife is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, so your donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

Can I donate Real Estate?

Yes, we accept real estate donations. Please contact our donations department at donations@seelife.org to discuss the process.

Can I donate Stock?

Yes, we accept stock donations. This can be a tax-efficient way to give. Contact us for instructions on how to transfer stock.

How can I mail my donation?

You can mail checks or money orders to: Seelife, 123 Charity Street, City, State, ZIP. Please include your contact information for a receipt.

How does Seelife use funds?

Over 85% of every dollar donated goes directly to program services. The remainder supports essential administrative and fundraising costs.
